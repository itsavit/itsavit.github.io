What we do

And these are some of the activities we conduct.

Prism of Possibilities

A descriptive workshop about How to present yourself during placements by Mr. Abhay Valsangkar was conducted.

Cyber Security Workshop

A workshop was organised for students to provide them the knowledge about website vulnerabilities using Burpsuite.

PHP Application Development

A descriptive workshop about development of websites using PHP technology by Mr. Kiran Sadvilkar was conducted.

Poster making event

Students created various E-posters pertaining to latest technology in IT field. The posters were their innovative ideas.

Android app development

A two day event was conducted for the students to learn the basics of android technology

Microsoft .NET Certification

Mr. Panchal (Microsoft Certified Trainer) guided students on an overall process of .NET certification.

Gadget Guru Seminar

Information on latest gadgets like Iphone6, Apple Watch, Xiaomi MI4 was showed through an excellent video

LAN Gaming

LAN gaming sessions were conducted for the students and the members. This session was fun and very interactive

Calculator Workshop

Students were taught to solve differentiation, integration, matrix and vector calculations via calculator

Cloud computing sessions

The primary idea of working of Cloud Computing, such as how the data is stored and retrieved in a cloud was explained.

Greek Olympiad Event

Interesting games for students in the Verve-2014 were organized under this event. Students and teachers participated together
